How alternatives are compared
  • Compare calculates a weighted score across cost, quality, compliance, and operational metrics for each alternative vs. the current AI agent.
  • 5-Year TCO = Setup Cost + (Monthly Cost x 12 x 5) + (Annual Maintenance x 5) — projected total cost of ownership.

  • Ongoing Annual Cost = Monthly Cost x 12 + Annual Maintenance — recurring yearly spend after Year 1.
  • Higher accuracy, scalability, compliance scores and lower error rate, deploy time favor the alternative.

How audit ROI is calculated
Audit Hours Saved = (Manual Hours − Agent Hours) × Hourly Rate
Risk Reduction Value = (Baseline Failure Risk − Agent Failure Risk) × Potential Penalty
Response Time Improvement = Baseline Days − Agent Days

Example: Audit failure risk drops from 10% to 0.1%, response time from 40 days to 2 days, confidence rises from 60% to 95% — quantifying agent impact on compliance readiness.

How Monte Carlo simulation works
  • For each parameter, define a Min, Likely (most probable), and Max value range.
  • The simulation runs N iterations, sampling random values within each range using a triangular distribution.
  • Results show the probability distribution of ROI outcomes, confidence intervals, and which parameters have the most impact.
  • Tornado chart ranks parameters by their correlation to the final ROI — higher correlation = more influence on the result.

How ROI is calculated
Labor Saved = Manual Hours × Hourly Rate × Avg Agent Executions
Net ROI = Labor Saved − Actual Agent LLM Cost (from runs)

Example: 1h manual work at $50/h, done 10 times = $500 manual cost. If the agent spent $5 in LLM calls, net savings = $495.

How compliance risk is calculated
Compliance Upside = Fine Exposure × Failure Probability
Risk Costs = Vendor Lock-in + Governance Overhead
Risk Benefits = Operational Risk Reduction + Turnover Savings

Example: $100K fine exposure at 10% probability = $10K avoided risk. Add $5K operational savings, minus $2K governance overhead = $13K net compliance value.

How costs & benefits are calculated
Total Costs = LLM Cost + Infrastructure Cost + Error Remediation
Total Benefits = Labor Saved + Revenue + Audit Avoided + Penalty Risk Avoided
Net ROI = Total Benefits − Total Costs

Example: $500 labor saved + $200 audit avoided = $700 benefits. Minus $50 LLM cost + $30 infrastructure = $620 net ROI.
